## Business Description

Maison Remodeling is a full-service residential remodeling and renovation company specializing in high-quality construction, design, and property revitalization. We help homeowners, investors, and real estate professionals transform outdated or damaged properties into modern, functional, and high-value homes.

Our services include complete home renovations, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, flooring, painting, carpentry, structural upgrades, project management, and post-project warranty follow-ups. We handle every phase—from initial planning and budgeting to execution and final walkthrough—ensuring a smooth, transparent, and professional experience.
