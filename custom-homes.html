Custom Home Building FAQs

The timeline for building a custom home typically ranges from 10-18 months, depending on the size and complexity of the project. This includes the design phase (2-4 months), permitting (1-3 months), and construction (8-12 months). We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase so you know exactly what to expect.

Custom home construction costs in San Jose typically range from $400-$800+ per square foot, depending on the level of finishes, architectural complexity, and site conditions. A mid-range custom home usually costs $500-$600 per square foot, while luxury homes with premium finishes can exceed $800 per square foot. We provide detailed estimates tailored to your specific project.

While we don't sell land directly, we can help evaluate potential lots for buildability, assess site conditions, and advise on any challenges that might affect construction costs. We also have relationships with local real estate professionals who specialize in land sales and can provide referrals.

Yes, changes can be made during construction through our change order process. However, changes made after construction begins may affect your timeline and budget. We work hard during the design phase to finalize all details, but we understand that sometimes adjustments are needed. We'll provide cost and timeline impacts for any requested changes.

Yes, we stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. We offer a 1-year warranty on workmanship covering any defects in construction, a 2-year warranty on mechanical systems (HVAC, plumbing, electrical), and a 10-year structural warranty. Additionally, many materials and appliances come with their own manufacturer warranties.

We offer many energy-efficient features including solar panel integration, high-efficiency HVAC systems, advanced insulation, energy-efficient windows and doors, LED lighting, tankless water heaters, and smart home systems for energy management. California's building codes already require energy-efficient construction, and we can help you exceed these standards for even greater savings.
