Design-Build FAQ

Design-build is a project delivery method where a single company provides both design services (architectural plans, interior design) and construction services. Instead of hiring an architect and contractor separately, you work with one integrated team that handles everything from initial concept through final construction. This creates a more streamlined, efficient, and collaborative process.

Design-build is often more cost-effective because budgets are established early with builder input, reducing costly mid-project changes. You also save money by avoiding separate fees for architects and contractors, and projects typically finish faster, reducing labor costs. Studies show design-build projects are completed 33% faster and cost 6% less on average than traditional methods.
