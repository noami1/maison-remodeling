A typical kitchen remodel takes 6-12 weeks depending on the scope of work. Minor updates may take 2-4 weeks, while full gut renovations with structural changes can take 12-16 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the planning phase.

Kitchen remodel costs vary widely based on size, materials, and scope. A minor remodel might start around $25,000, while a mid-range remodel typically runs $50,000-$80,000. High-end custom kitchens can exceed $100,000. We provide free detailed estimates.
